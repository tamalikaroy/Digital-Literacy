

| Do's | Don'ts |
| :---- | :---- |
| **Do** audit your privacy settings regularly to control who sees your content. | **Don't** post confidential information about your university or workplace. |
| **Do** engage in respectful discourse, even when you disagree with others. | **Don't** post impulsive comments or "vent" during a moment of frustration. |
| **Do** showcase your projects (GitHub, Kaggle) to build a professional brand. | **Don't** share unverified news or "deepfake" content without checking facts. |
| **Do** use a professional profile picture on networking sites like LinkedIn. | **Don't** tag colleagues or professors in unprofessional memes or photos. |
| **Do** think of the "Google Test"—only post what you'd want a recruiter to see. | **Don't** ignore the impact of your "digital footprint" on your future career. |
